Blue Sky Art Studio

Aug 2017 | 6 Weeks

Role | User research, ideation, identity design, interface design

Blue Sky Art Classroom is an art studio supplying various art classes for all ages. The art classes include painting, music and dance etc. I was asked to help with everything from branding to website.

Jenny is the founder and manager of Blue Sky Art Classroom. She hopes all the designs are inviting, vibrant, and creative. Jenny asked me to build them a website that showcases their art classes. The site includes a booking system where their customers can register classes and make a payment.

Before this, Jenny used a paper-based registration form to record the class attendance and payment. According to Jenny’s registered form, I analysize the gender and age of the attendants.

According to the chart we can obviously see there is a high concentration in female users.

I interviewed 34 users and the most important information they hope to get in the website are: 1) class introduction 2) instructor introduction 3)weekly schedule 4) available spots 5) cost of per class.

Based on what I learned, I created a site map to show the structure of the website to work out how I want visitors to use and navigate around. The main design principle of my design is to ensure content is in places users would expect to find it.

Homepage of a website plays the central role in showcasing brand value. I would like the users to be attracted and appealed at the first glance on the homepage—which, in my mind, is he bridge between designers and users. My homepage design follows three design tips: DISPLAY THE IDENTITY CLEARLY, LESS IS MORE, and EASY TO USE.

For the first time visitors, they will get to know the studio by a 3-min video. Jenny’s information and welcome will also be seen on this “ABOUT US” page.

CLASS INTRODUCTION includes the introduction of classes, introduction of instructors, schedule, whether open spots are available for the recent two weeks. Class registration button are on the bottom to navigate the visitors to the registration page.

Visitors are welcome to SIGN UP to be a studio member anytime by clicking the sign in/up button on the right top corner. The visual design of the SIGN UP page is pleasant, simple and clean.

I designed Blue Sky Art Studio’s Logo, posters, business cards among other printed material.

Jenny hopes the logo is unique, memorable and attractive, and it can make an instant connect with the audience. I designed a cloud with rainbow colors hidden in it. It means that in Blue Sky Art Studio, people can release their artistic creativity and it will color their real life.

These very feelings or emotions will be the message that I want to deliver to my target customers through your logo design.
