Bull Horn Hotpot

Aug 2018 | 4 Weeks

Role | User research, Interface Design, Graphic Design

Bull Horn Hotpot specializes in Taiwanese hot soup cuisine. The restaurant offers various mini hotpots on their menu. They first swung open its doors in 2013, and in recent year they experience a skyrocketing increase in take-out orders by phone. In 2018, the restaurant claims that more than 30% of their orders are takeout. Bull Horn Hotpot asked me to design a mobile APP to allows users place orders, pay ahead and pick it up in a store without waiting in line. The Bull Horn Hotpot expects their customer can enjoy the fast loading time, ease of use and delight during interaction from the APP.

This project shown here is a 4-week UI design of a restaurant Order-and-Pay Mobile APP. The objective of the design lies not only in ability to pay with smartphone for hotpot customers, as well as a rewards program.

My design is a process of discovering the behaviors, motivations and needs of the customers through observation, communication, task analysis, and other types of user feedback. The research helps me proactively look for steps in the user journey where users might need help.

Competitor Analysis is used to highlight strengths and weaknesses of products in order to make more informed decisions about my product strategy. In order to better understand this industry and find out where the product currently fit within the market I conducted a competitor analysis on the order-and-pay type of apps. Doing the competitor analysis help me knowing how to research properly and understand exactly what information you are looking for.

I gather information about users by observing them directly, which aims to understand the human side of data by gaining a sense of the underlying reasons and motivations surrounding consumer behavior.

I concluded the questions of WHO, WHERE, WHEN, and WHO. Additionally, I asked directly what they value in an order-and-payment product. The customer research helps me to design the product correctly the first time, instead of having to fix it later when customers are unhappy.

I created user flows for the customer ordering process. This helped ensure that each step was prioritized and obstacle-free. I made sure to include a clear entry point through a set of steps towards a successful outcome and final action.

The Bull Horn Hotpot wanted their APP to reflect the style of their restaurant. I also was responsible for designing their logo and printed menus.
